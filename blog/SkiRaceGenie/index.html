SkiRaceGenie

22 January, 2022 - 3 min read

Following the success of the Hackathon, the SkiRaceGenie team was contacted by FIS, who requested the game to be presented in Cortina D'Ampezzo. The game garnered significant attention and was even covered by major news outlets, including SkySport. It was presented on January 22nd during the female's Downhill race and on January 23rd during the female's SuperG, marking a huge success.
